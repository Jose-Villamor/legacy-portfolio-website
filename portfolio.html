03 : About me

Studied finance and worked in that field for some years. Then I had the opportunity to enter an MBA in International Business. During the master we had a conference on data and I got really interested in the topic. Some courses and a few applications later I got a data analyst job at Transcom.

For the past 3 years I have been studying and working in data centric matters. During this period I have acquired knowledge and experience in Data Analysis, Machine Learning, NLP, Statistical Analysis, Databases, AWS, Big data, Python, SQL, Tableau and Spark.

Personally, I feel a lot of drive, excitement and satisfaction working in the data sector as it has a huge impact in the world and it is constantly evolving.
